The Journey

Every photograph tells a story. From the icy landscapes of Iceland to the bustling streets of New York, my lens captures moments that transcend time. This dual-scroll effect symbolizes the interplay between stillness and motion, light and shadow.

About

Fakhriel Yusmana Shiddiq captures the raw essence of human emotion through monochrome. Based between Paris and New York, her work has been featured in Vogue, i-D, and The British Journal of Photography. With a career spanning over a decade, Anna's minimalist approach reveals the soul beneath the surface.
